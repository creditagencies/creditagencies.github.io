Best Buy Credit Card for Drones: Financing & Discounts

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Drones have become one of the most exciting and rapidly evolving technologies in recent years. From aerial photography to delivery services, their applications are endless. However, high-quality drones often come with a hefty price tag, making financing options like the Best Buy Credit Card an attractive solution for enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Why Consider the Best Buy Credit Card for Your Drone Purchase?

1. Exclusive Financing Options

The Best Buy Credit Card offers 0% APR financing for qualified purchases, allowing you to spread out payments over time without accruing interest. For drones that cost anywhere from $500 to $2,000+, this can be a game-changer.

  • 6- or 12-month financing on purchases over $199
  • 24-month financing on select premium drones (terms may vary)

2. Instant Discounts & Reward Points

Cardholders often receive 10% back in rewards on their first purchase or 5% back in rewards on future Best Buy purchases. These rewards can be redeemed for accessories like extra batteries, propellers, or even future tech upgrades.

3. Early Access to Sales & Promotions

Best Buy frequently runs Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and holiday sales on drones from brands like DJI, Autel, and Skydio. Cardholders get early access to these deals, ensuring they snag the best models before they sell out.

Top Drones to Buy with Best Buy Financing

1. DJI Mini 4 Pro – Best for Travel & Portability

  • Weight: Under 250g (no FAA registration required in the U.S.)
  • 4K/60fps video & 48MP photos
  • Omnidirectional obstacle avoidance

Financing Tip: Use the Best Buy Credit Card to break down the $759 price tag into manageable monthly payments.

2. Autel Robotics EVO Lite+ – Best for Professional Filmmaking

  • 6K HDR camera
  • 40-minute flight time
  • Advanced AI tracking

Why Finance? At $1,299, this drone is a significant investment—Best Buy’s 24-month financing makes it affordable.

3. Skydio 2+ – Best for Autonomous Flight

  • AI-powered obstacle avoidance
  • 4K60 video with HDR
  • No-fly-zone compliance

Rewards Bonus: Use your 5% back in rewards to buy an extra battery ($179 value).

How to Maximize Your Best Buy Credit Card for Drone Purchases

1. Stack Discounts with Seasonal Sales

Best Buy often offers "Deal of the Day" promotions or member-exclusive pricing. Pair these with your card’s financing for maximum savings.

2. Use My Best Buy Points for Accessories

Instead of paying out of pocket for ND filters, carrying cases, or memory cards, redeem your rewards points to cover these extras.

3. Upgrade with Trade-In Programs

Best Buy accepts drone trade-ins, which can be combined with your credit card rewards for an even bigger discount on your next model.

The Future of Drones & Why Financing Matters

With advancements in AI, longer battery life, and stricter FAA regulations, drones are becoming more sophisticated—and more expensive. Financing options like the Best Buy Credit Card make cutting-edge technology accessible without draining your savings.

Whether you're a hobbyist capturing stunning landscapes or a business owner using drones for inspections, the right financing plan can help you stay ahead in this fast-growing industry.

So, if you’ve been eyeing that high-end drone but hesitated due to cost, the Best Buy Credit Card might just be the key to unlocking your next aerial adventure.
