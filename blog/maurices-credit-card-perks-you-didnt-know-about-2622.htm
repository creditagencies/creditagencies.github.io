Maurices Credit Card Perks You Didn’t Know About

Home / Blog / Blog Details

When it comes to retail credit cards, many shoppers focus on the obvious benefits—discounts, points, or cashback. But what if your Maurices credit card could do more than just save you money on your next boho-chic outfit? In today’s fast-paced world, where financial flexibility and sustainability matter more than ever, unlocking hidden perks can make a real difference. From exclusive sustainability initiatives to unexpected travel benefits, here’s a deep dive into the lesser-known advantages of your Maurices credit card.

1. Eco-Conscious Rewards: Fashion Meets Sustainability

Shop Green, Earn More

Did you know your Maurices credit card rewards you for making eco-friendly purchases? The brand has quietly rolled out a "Sustainable Style Bonus" program. When you buy from Maurices’ eco-conscious collections (like their recycled fabric lines or vegan leather accessories), you earn double points compared to regular purchases.

Carbon Offset Partnerships

Maurices has partnered with reforestation initiatives and carbon offset programs. Cardholders who opt into paperless billing automatically contribute to planting trees—without spending extra. Check your account dashboard to see how many trees your account has helped plant!

2. Financial Flexibility in Tough Times

Interest-Free Grace Period Extensions

With inflation and economic uncertainty affecting budgets, Maurices offers unpublished hardship programs. If you’re facing financial strain, calling customer service could unlock temporary 0% APR extensions or waived late fees. This perk isn’t advertised, but it’s a lifeline for loyal customers.

"Buy Now, Pay Later" Without Extra Apps

Skip third-party services like Klarna—your Maurices card already has a built-in installment plan. For purchases over $100, you can split payments into 4 interest-free chunks by requesting it at checkout (online or in-store).

3. Surprise Travel and Lifestyle Perks

Discounted Airport Parking

Frequent traveler? Maurices cardholders get 15% off at Park ‘N Fly locations nationwide. Just book through the card’s online portal—a little-known hack for jet-setters.

Exclusive Access to Local Events

Maurices occasionally hosts cardholder-only styling workshops or pop-up sales. These aren’t widely publicized; you’ll only get invites if your account is opted into emails. Pro tip: Update your preferences to avoid missing out.

4. Hidden Discounts Beyond Clothing

Partnered Wellness Deals

Your card unlocks discounts at select wellness brands, like 20% off at Saje Natural Wellness or Headspace subscriptions. These partnerships rotate quarterly, so check the "Offers" tab in your account.

Gas and Grocery Cashback (Yes, Really!)

While the card’s main rewards are fashion-focused, using it for gas or groceries during promotional periods earns 3% cashback instead of the usual 1%. Look for seasonal announcements—especially around holidays.

5. Community Impact You Didn’t Know You Were Making

Automatic Donations to Women’s Shelters

Every time you use your Maurices card, a micro-donation (rounded up from your purchase) goes to Dress for Success and similar nonprofits. You can adjust the amount or opt out, but it’s enabled by default—meaning your shopping already supports a cause.

Volunteer Hour Rewards

Participate in a Maurices-sponsored charity event (like a clothing drive)? Submit proof of volunteer hours via the app to earn 500 bonus points per hour. It’s their way of rewarding kindness.

6. Tech-Savvy Features for the Digital Age

Virtual Card Numbers for Safe Online Shopping

Worried about data breaches? Generate a one-time virtual card number for online purchases via the Maurices app. It’s a safer alternative to sharing your actual card details.

Alexa and Google Assistant Integration

Manage your account hands-free by linking it to Alexa or Google Assistant. Ask, “What’s my Maurices rewards balance?” or “When’s my payment due?” without logging in.

Final Thought: Maximizing Your Card’s Potential

The key to unlocking these perks? Stay curious and engaged. Regularly check your account’s "Benefits" section, enable notifications, and don’t hesitate to ask customer service, “What’s new?” Your Maurices card isn’t just a payment tool—it’s a gateway to smarter, more meaningful spending.
