The Best Credit Union Perks at 717 Credit Union

Home / Blog / Blog Details

In today's rapidly evolving economic landscape, where global inflation, climate anxiety, and digital security threats dominate headlines, finding a financial partner that offers more than just basic banking is not a luxury—it's a necessity. For members of communities in Pennsylvania and beyond, 717 Credit Union stands as a beacon of stability and value. Unlike massive, impersonal megabanks, this member-owned cooperative is designed to prioritize people over profits. The perks of membership here are not merely transactional; they are a comprehensive toolkit designed to empower individuals and families to navigate modern challenges and build a more secure future.

Navigating Economic Uncertainty with Superior Rates and Lower Fees

With rising costs of living straining budgets globally, every dollar saved is a victory. 717 Credit Union directly addresses this pain point with a structure inherently geared toward saving members money.

High-Yield Savings Accounts that Actually Fight Inflation

While traditional banks offer minuscule savings rates that lose value to inflation, 717 Credit Union's high-yield savings accounts provide a competitive Annual Percentage Yield (APY). This allows your emergency fund or future savings to grow more effectively, creating a crucial buffer against economic shocks. It’s a fundamental strategy for personal financial resilience.

Loan Rates That Don't Break the Bank

Whether financing a new vehicle, consolidating high-interest credit card debt, or investing in a home, 717 offers loan rates that are consistently lower than those of national banks. This translates to significantly lower monthly payments and less money paid in interest over the life of the loan. In an era of rising interest rates, this perk is more valuable than ever.

The Radical Advantage of Fewer and Lower Fees

Overdraft fees, ATM fees, monthly maintenance fees—these are the profit centers of big banks. At 717 Credit Union, these fees are either drastically reduced or eliminated entirely. Members enjoy access to a vast network of surcharge-free ATMs and receive reimbursements for fees incurred at other institutions' machines. This commitment to fee-free banking puts hundreds of dollars back into members' pockets annually.

Embracing the Digital Revolution with Secure and Innovative Tools

The future of finance is digital, but it must also be secure. 717 Credit Union invests in top-tier technology to provide convenience without compromising safety.

A Mobile Banking Experience Designed for Modern Life

Their robust mobile app is a command center for your finances. Features include seamless mobile check deposit, instant bill pay, peer-to-peer (P2P) payments, and real-time fraud alerts. You can freeze a lost card with a single tap, a critical feature for preventing financial crime in a digital world.

Financial Health Dashboards and Budgeting Tools

Understanding your cash flow is the first step to controlling it. The credit union’s online banking platform often includes integrated tools that categorize spending, track net worth, and help set and achieve savings goals. This empowers members to make data-driven financial decisions.

Proactive Cybersecurity Measures

In response to growing cyber threats, 717 employs advanced encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and continuous monitoring to protect member data. They also prioritize member education on recognizing phishing scams and protecting personal information, creating a first line of defense.

Building Community Resilience Through Local Focus and Support

In a world facing climate-related disasters and social fragmentation, the strength of local community is paramount. As a not-for-profit institution, 717 Credit Union's mission is intrinsically linked to the prosperity of its local region.

Investing in Local People and Small Businesses

The deposits made by members are reinvested in the form of loans to other members within the community—for homes, small businesses, and education. This creates a virtuous cycle where your money helps your neighbor start a business, which creates local jobs and strengthens the regional economy. This is the opposite of the extractive model of large banks, which often invest deposits in distant markets.

Financial Literacy Programs for All Ages

Combating financial inequality starts with education. 717 Credit Union often hosts free workshops on topics like first-time home buying, retirement planning, and credit management for teens and young adults. By equipping members with knowledge, they build a more financially literate and resilient community.

Support in Times of Crisis

Credit unions are renowned for their personal touch, especially during difficult times. Whether a member is facing a job loss, a medical emergency, or the aftermath of a local disaster, 717 Credit Union has programs in place to provide support, such as loan payment deferrals or hardship assistance. This human-centric approach is a priceless perk that large institutions simply cannot replicate.

Promoting Sustainable Choices with Green Financial Products

Climate change is a defining issue of our time, and many individuals seek ways to align their financial choices with their environmental values. 717 Credit Union offers pathways to do just that.

Discounts on Eco-Friendly Loans

Many credit unions, including 717, offer special incentives for "green" initiatives. This can include discounted interest rates on auto loans for purchasing hybrid or electric vehicles (EVs) or on personal loans for making energy-efficient home improvements like solar panel installation, new insulation, or high-efficiency HVAC systems.

Paperless Banking Incentives

To reduce environmental impact, 717 encourages members to opt for electronic statements and online correspondence, sometimes offering small monetary incentives or entry into prize drawings for doing so. This simple switch reduces waste and the carbon footprint associated with paper mail.

Beyond Banking: Exclusive Member-Only Benefits

The value of membership extends into everyday life with discounts and services that provide unexpected value.

Insurance and Warranty Programs

Members often have access to discounted rates on auto and home insurance through partnerships with trusted providers. Additionally, many credit unions offer exclusive extended warranty programs for vehicles purchased with an auto loan and cell phone protection plans when the phone bill is paid with a 717 credit union debit or credit card.

Travel and Entertainment Discounts

Through their service networks, members can access discounts on rental cars, hotel stays, and even theme park tickets. These perks make leisure travel more affordable, adding a layer of value that enhances quality of life.

The true power of 717 Credit Union lies in its holistic approach. It’s not just a place to store money; it’s a partner committed to enhancing your financial well-being, securing your digital life, strengthening your community, and even supporting your personal values. In a complex and often uncertain world, these comprehensive perks provide a roadmap to not just surviving, but thriving.
